How Camp Claude works
  1. Show up to the monthly Learning Lab — live or watch the recording the next day. That's the main event. Brandon teaches one focused topic per month, builds something real, and takes questions.
  2. Try the Skill Builders when something interesting drops — these are ad hoc 10–30 min how-tos whenever there's something worth showing. No schedule. Watch on demand.
  3. Hang out at The Campfires — the member-to-member feed where people share what they're building, ask what they're stuck on, and post wins. Brandon shows up here too.
  4. Save what works to your Library — the AI Resource Center compounds as you stay. Skills, templates, connector maps, and prompt stacks — all organized by department and level.
