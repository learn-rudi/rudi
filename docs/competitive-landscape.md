# Competitive Landscape — Camp Claude

*Consolidated competitive intel for Camp Claude positioning. Last updated 2026-05-17. Pulls from 5 audited landing pages, 22-creator YouTube research dataset, and 8 Skool community deep-dives. Living document — append as new competitors surface.*

---

## TL;DR — what you need to remember

1. **Your $99/mo Camp Claude price is the category median.** $97–$99/mo is where Claude/AI communities cluster (Claude Club, Chase AI+, AAS+, Claude Club, AI Builders all at this point). $49/mo founder pricing matches the discount competitors offer (Brock's locked rate is $45/mo).

2. **Your #1 direct threat is Brock Mesarich** ("AI for Non-Techies," $67/mo, 835 members). Same audience, same tool focus, real YouTube momentum (105K subs).

3. **The disruption threat is Claude Code Club** (Duncan Rogoff, $9/mo lifetime lock, 4,000 members). Land-grab pricing on a category-defining name. Different audience (beginner makers) but sits on Claude SEO real estate.

4. **Claude Cowork is unclaimed.** Across 13+ competitors and 4,160 YouTube videos analyzed, *no one* has staked the flag as "the Cowork community." 90-day window before this closes.

5. **Your moat is RUDI Academy positioning + framework IP + named enterprise credibility.** Every competitor sells either "build an agency" or "land a client" or "ship an app." Nobody sells "become the most AI-fluent professional in your organization." That's your lane.

## White space — what Camp Claude can own

### 1. "Professional fluency" framing (not "build an agency")

Every paid AI community in the dataset sells one of three things:
- **Build an AI agency** (Nate Herk, Liam Ottley, Nick Saraev, Chase AI+, Maker School)
- **Land your first client** (Maker School, Chase AI+, parts of AAS+)
- **Ship an app** (Vibe Coding, Claude Code Club, AI Builders)

**Nobody** sells *"become the most AI-fluent professional in your organization."*

That's Camp Claude's lane: the *employed knowledge worker* (FP&A, marketing, HR, ops, consulting, real estate) who wants to AI-augment their existing job — not start a new AI business.

### 2. Cowork as primary identity (90-day window)

Across 13+ competitors and 4,160 YouTube videos:
- Brock Mesarich teaches Cowork but his brand is generic ("AI for Non-Techies")
- Claude Club covers Cowork but is broader (Claude + OpenClaw)
- Clief Notes touches Cowork but focuses on architecture
- **No community is positioned as "the Cowork community"**

Cowork launched January 2026. The 90-day window before someone else claims it is closing.

**Action:** Bake "Cowork" into the Camp Claude hero copy. Own the term.

### 3. Multi-tool / multi-camp architecture

Every direct competitor is single-tool:
- Nate Herk = n8n
- Nick Saraev = Make.com
- Chase Hannegan = Claude Code
- Brock = Claude
- Claude Club = Claude + OpenClaw

**Camp Claude's "Camp Claude → Camp Codex → Camp Gemini → Custom Camps" architecture is structurally differentiated.** Don't downplay it.

### 4. School / Academy framing

Every winner positions as:
- A "course" (Augment, Vibe Coding)
- A "community" (Brock, AAS+, Liam)
- A "workshop" (Pareto, AdVenture)
- An "accelerator" (Liam's AAA)

**No one positions as a "school" or "academy."** RUDI Academy + AI Training Camps + Camp Claude as signature program is institutional positioning nobody else has.

### 5. Free top-of-funnel + paid premium (deferred)

Nate Herk: 330K free + 3,700 paid ($99/mo).
Liam Ottley: 317K free + 3,800 paid ($5K–$7K).

Pattern: **free Skool community feeding paid tier** is the dominant winning structure.

**Brandon doesn't have a free Skool community yet.** Plan for Month 6+: spin up a free Camp Claude Skool (low-effort, no paid deliverables) as an evergreen lead generator.

## Patterns to avoid

### Pricing

- ❌ Don't go below $49/mo (Brock's annual locked rate is the floor)
- ❌ Don't go above $120/mo without a client-acquisition promise (Maker School at $184 requires "land your first client" outcome)
- ❌ Don't add a separate Bootcamp tier — it's redundant with Camp Claude membership (confirmed in spec)
- ❌ Don't offer "lifetime" pricing as the primary plan (Claude Code Club's $9 lifetime is a land-grab strategy, not a sustainable model for Camp Claude's positioning)

### Positioning

- ❌ Don't position as "build an AI agency" — saturated by Nate (729K), Nick (415K), Liam (782K)
- ❌ Don't position as "land your first client" — saturated by Maker School, Chase AI+
- ❌ Don't position as "ship apps with AI" — saturated by Vibe Coding, Claude Code Club
- ❌ Don't try to out-tutorial Brock Mesarich on Claude Cowork mechanics

- ❌ Don't run free community at launch (deferred to Month 6+)
