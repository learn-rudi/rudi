THE AUTOMATION LADDER

Where you are, and what's at the top.

Most professionals are stuck on rungs 2 or 3 — using AI as a chat assistant or directing it to do small things. The top rung — where Claude runs as a digital employee on a schedule — is what $20/mo unlocks. The workshop shows you the climb.

01

Manual

You do everything yourself. The default.

02

Chat Assistant

Claude helps you think, draft, brainstorm. Free Claude. Where most people stop.

03

Chat-Directed

You direct Claude to act in a chat — "send this email," "summarize my Drive." Free + connectors.

04

Agented (Digital Employee)

Claude runs autonomously on a schedule. Cowork. Requires Claude Pro ($20/mo).

WHAT YOU'LL BUILD

Three reusable Claude Skills. Yours to keep.

By the end of the workshop, you'll have three structured prompt files ("Skills") you can drop into Claude (or any AI tool) and reuse forever. Each one solves a specific people-leader task.

SKILL 01

1:1 Prep

Generate a focused prep doc for any 1:1 in 30 seconds — what they'll bring up, what you should bring up, one open-ended question.

SKILL 02

Performance Feedback

Draft direct, kind, specific feedback in your voice — 3 paragraphs, ready to edit.

SKILL 03

Difficult Conversation Prep

Plan a hard conversation — opening line, anticipated objections, your boundary, the exit line.
