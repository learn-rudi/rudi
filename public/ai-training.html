THE PROGRESSION

Three stages of AI fluency.

Most professionals are stuck at stage one. The camps move you through all three.

01 Around

AI happening around you

You hear about it, see it in tools, watch it change your industry. You're not driving it yet.

02 Collaborating

AI collaborating with you

You're using chat interfaces. Asking questions, drafting documents, brainstorming.

03 Alongside

AI working alongside you

Agents and automations doing real work in your account. You delegate. They ship.

HOW IT WORKS

Inside every camp.

The structure is the same across camps. The tool and the curriculum change.

01

Live campfires

Recurring sessions hosted by Brandon. Tonight's campfire might be on connectors, plugins, or a vertical-specific automation.

02

Async community

Members-only Circle space. Ask questions, share automations, get feedback between campfires.

03

Library

Every past campfire, every template, every working automation. An asset that compounds as you stay.

04

Vertical sub-tracks

When you're ready: Real Estate, Finance, Small Business sub-communities inside the main camp.
