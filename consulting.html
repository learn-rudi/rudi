Engagement Process

Four phases to AI capability

A structured approach that moves your organization from assessment to implementation with clear deliverables at each stage.

Phase 01

Discovery & Foundation

Understand your organization's relationship with AI—where you are, where you want to go, and what's in the way.

Inputs

  • Discovery call
  • Educational foundation
  • Stakeholder alignment
→ Readiness Baseline
Phase 02

Diagnosis & Assessment

Measure AI literacy across your organization. Identify hotspots, risks, and high-impact opportunity areas.

Inputs

  • Data analysis
  • Anonymous AI readiness surveys
  • Role-based interviews
→ Hotspots & Focus Areas
Phase 03

Workflow Mapping

Map AI opportunities to actual work. Identify entry points, prioritize use cases, and design integration paths.

Inputs

  • Entry point assessment
  • Literacy framework mapping
  • Workflow analysis
→ Integration Plan
Phase 04

Training & Implementation

Execute the plan. Train leadership, enable departments, and deploy tools with proper governance in place.

Outputs

  • Executive training
  • Department enablement
  • Tool deployment
→ Organizational Capability

Our Approach

We don't sell tools. We build capability.

Most AI consultants focus on technology. We focus on people—building the judgment, skills, and governance structures that make AI adoption sustainable.

The result isn't just higher adoption rates. It's lasting organizational capability—the ability to integrate new AI systems wisely as they emerge.

  • Literacy before tools — We build understanding, not dependency
  • Readiness before training — Psychological comfort precedes technical competency
  • Governance as foundation — Sustainable innovation requires clear guardrails
  • People + Technology — Both pillars matter equally
  • Measured outcomes — We track adoption, time saved, and risk reduction
