# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

RUDI is committed to providing a welcoming, inclusive, and professional environment for everyone interested in responsible AI education.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and considerate in all interactions
- Focus on constructive dialogue and learning
- Accept responsibility for mistakes and learn from them
- Prioritize what is best for the community
- Support diverse perspectives and backgrounds

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling or deliberately inflammatory comments
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Disruptive behavior in learning environments
- Other conduct inappropriate in a professional/educational setting

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:
- All RUDI online spaces (website, forums, social media)
- RUDI events and training sessions
- Interactions between community members

## Enforcement

Community leaders are responsible for clarifying standards and taking appropriate action in response to unacceptable behavior.

Reports of unacceptable behavior may be submitted through the website contact form. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
