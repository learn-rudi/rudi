Upskill Ohio Coalition

RUDI is a founding member of Upskill Ohio, a coalition of Ohio business leaders committed to preparing 10,000 workers for the AI economy by leveraging state TechCred funding.

In partnership with Did You Know Publishing, Inc., we provide comprehensive AI training and TechCred-eligible certification programs to Ohio employers.

State Funding

$25.2M available for Ohio employers

RUDI certifications are TechCred-eligible. With six bi-monthly application windows, employers can train up to 90 employees per year—100% reimbursed by the state.
