How It Works

Describe. Direct. Create.

You bring the thinking. AI helps with the execution. One interface, no context-switching.

Step 01

Express your intent

Tell Studio what you want to create, analyze, summarize, or research. Plain language. No special syntax required.

Step 02

AI assists

Studio works with your documents, applies the right AI model, and helps you accomplish the task. You stay in control.

Step 03

Iterate and refine

Review results, ask follow-up questions, refine your approach. Everything stays in context as you work.
