Training Framework

Three levels of AI maturity

We train teams from AI happening around them → to AI collaborating with them → to AI working alongside them.

Level 01

Ambient AI

Passive Use

AI is already embedded in tools your team uses daily—search, email, documents, social platforms. Most people don't recognize it.

Outcomes

  • Recognize AI outputs in everyday tools
  • Reduce misinformation and hallucination risk
  • Establish baseline AI use policies
  • Build verification habits
Level 02

Chat Interfaces

Active Collaboration

ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity—direct conversation with AI models. This is where most organizations are trying to start (and struggling).

Outcomes

  • Prompting as communication skill
  • Task decomposition patterns
  • Reusable templates and workflows
  • Quality assurance habits
Level 03

Agents + Workflows

Applied Automation

AI assistants that execute multi-step tasks, call tools, follow processes, and integrate into real work. This is where RUDI Studio becomes essential.

Outcomes

  • Workflow design and automation
  • Role-based AI assistants
  • Human-in-the-loop guardrails
  • Measurable time savings

Responsible AI

People + Technology

Across all three levels, we teach Responsible AI through two pillars—so adoption is useful, safe, and sustainable.

P People

  • AI literacy and judgment development
  • Bias awareness and responsible decision-making
  • Communication norms (when to use AI, when not to)
  • Change management and adoption design
  • Verification and quality habits

T Technology

  • Data privacy and sensitive information handling
  • Governance guardrails and access controls
  • Tool selection criteria and risk tiers
  • Workflow design for safe automation
  • Monitoring, logging, and audit trails
